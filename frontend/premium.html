Frequently Asked Questions

How does insurance integration work?

You'll securely connect your insurance information, and MAI will search your network for covered mental health providers, medications, and treatments in your area.

What if I don't have insurance?

MAI will help you find community health centers, sliding-scale therapy options, and affordable medication programs in your area.

How does human customer service work?

You'll have access to real wellness advocates who can review your AI recommendations, help with phone calls to providers, and assist with paperwork and scheduling.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Monthly plans can be canceled anytime. 3-month and annual plans can be canceled with 30 days notice.
